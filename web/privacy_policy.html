Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TripMe.ai. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at support@tripme.ai.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for the app, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when participating in activities within the app or otherwise contacting us.

  • Personal Data: Email address, display name, and profile picture (if provided via Google Sign-In).
  • Location Data: We request access to your device's location to provide personalized "Discovery" and "Local Gems" features. This data is processed locally and via secure cloud services to enhance your experience.
  • Trips & Plans: Metadata about your origins, destinations, and interests is processed by the "Oracle AI" to generate itineraries.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our application.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our application features (AI Oracle).
  • To understand and analyze how you use our application.
  • To develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • To communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners.

Data Protection & Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. All sensitive data (cached plans, interests) is stored using AES Encryption via Hive and Flutter Secure Storage.

Your Rights (Account Deletion)

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the Profile Screen of the application.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses several third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics
  • Google Maps API
